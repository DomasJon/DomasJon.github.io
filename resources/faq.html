FAQ (Freguently Asked Questions)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

• Open the following link in your browser: https://hz.mynetpay.nl/
• Log in with your HZ username and password
• Tap Web Print
• At Step 1: Select a printer, check the settings (format, single/double sided etc.)
• Click in the field at Step 2: Select files to choose your files (you can also drag and drop)
• Click on Upload files now

Your file has now been sent to the appropriate printer. Use your HZ card at the printer to print the document.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Scanning to a private/external mailbox is not possible.

• Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ user name & password.
• Choose the Scan option in the menu.
• Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is available at the car park at Poelendaelesingel 12 in Middelburg. This is at a few minutes’ walk from HZ. Unfortunately, it is not possible to park directly at the HZ premises. However, there is a disabled parking space available right next to the HZ building at Het Groene woud 1-3.
